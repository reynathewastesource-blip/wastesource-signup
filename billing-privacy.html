Waste Source Billing — Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

Waste Source Billing is an internal application used by The Waste Source to prepare customer invoices. It connects to The Waste Source's own QuickBooks Online accounts through Intuit's official interfaces, with authorization granted by our QuickBooks administrator.

The application reads customer names, product names and prices from our QuickBooks accounts, and records invoices there. This information belongs to The Waste Source and is used only for billing our customers. The application does not sell, share, or disclose any data to third parties, does not use data for advertising, and does not collect information about members of the public.

Connection credentials are stored on The Waste Source's own computers. Access to QuickBooks can be revoked at any time by our administrator from the QuickBooks account settings. Questions about this policy may be directed to admin@thewastesource.com.
