Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

The Waste Source ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including your phone number and email address, in connection with our automated communication services.

📵 We will never sell, share, or rent your mobile phone number or personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you open an account with us:

  • Full name and business name
  • Phone number (mobile and/or business)
  • Email address
  • Billing and account information
  • Service usage data (locations visited, loads disposed)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending account notifications and billing reminders
  • Delivering automated SMS text messages regarding your account status
  • Sending automated phone call notices regarding past due balances
  • Sending email communications about your account
  • Providing customer service and account support

3. SMS Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and opening an account with The Waste Source, you consent to receive automated SMS text messages from us regarding your account. These messages may include:

  • Account balance notifications
  • Payment reminders and past due notices
  • Account suspension warnings
  • General account updates

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on account activity.

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us. To request help, reply HELP. After opting out you will no longer receive automated text messages from us.

4. Phone Calls

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive automated phone calls from The Waste Source regarding your account status, including past due balance notices and account suspension warnings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or mobile phone number with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our communication systems (such as messaging platforms), who are bound by confidentiality agreements

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.
